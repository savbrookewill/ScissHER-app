# Privacy Policy for ScissHER

**Last Updated: January 22, 2026**

## Introduction

ScissHER ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Profile Information**: Name, age, location, photos, bio, and preferences
- **Account Information**: Email address, phone number (if provided)
- **Identity Verification**: Age verification data

### Usage Information
- **App Activity**: Interactions, matches, messages, events attended
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Location Data**: Approximate location (with your permission)

### Photos and Media
- Profile photos and other images you upload to the app
- Camera access (only when you choose to take photos)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the ScissHER service
- Match you with other users based on preferences
- Facilitate communication between matched users
- Send notifications about matches, messages, and events
- Improve app features and user experience
- Ensure safety and prevent fraud or abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## AI and Data Processing

ScissHER uses Google's Gemini AI to:
- Generate personalized content and recommendations
- Enhance matching algorithms
- Provide conversational features

**Important**: We do not share personally identifiable information with AI services without anonymization.

### We Share Information With:
- **Other Users**: Your profile information is visible to matched users
- **Service Providers**: Third-party vendors who help us operate the app
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
